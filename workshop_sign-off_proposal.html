GDB Logistics operates MyFreight across three countries — France, Germany, and the Netherlands — without a shared operational standard. Each country has developed its own working practices, creating inconsistency in how shipments are managed, how work is distributed, and how issues are identified and resolved. This workshop exists to fix that. The objective is to define and agree a single global Ocean Import process in MyFreight, with country-specific exceptions permitted only where there is a clear operational justification. The Netherlands go-live in June 2025 makes this work time-critical: NL must launch on a standard that France and Germany will also adopt, not one built in isolation.
